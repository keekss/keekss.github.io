I began this project earlier this year after having the idea for a "depth-based" puzzle game.  The player has X "activations" to make at each "depth level", each node activated leads to other nodes, all of which have some relation to the "final node(s)" at greatest depths, which signal solving of the puzzle.

I am currently using Swift in XCode to implement the game, and it is still in the early stages.  My intent is for it to be a free game to offer to family, friends, and whoever else would like to try it.  I have play-tested several puzzles on paper and testers seem to enjoy the puzzle structure.  I hope to having a fully operating version by the end of the year!
